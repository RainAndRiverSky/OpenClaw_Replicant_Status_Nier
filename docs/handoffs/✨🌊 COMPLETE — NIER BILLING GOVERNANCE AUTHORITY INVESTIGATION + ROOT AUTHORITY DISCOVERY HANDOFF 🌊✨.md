4. Entitlement Authority Investigation

Root Architecture Discovery

The most important discovery of the entire research branch:

The identified gaps are not independent.

They are symptoms of a deeper governance concern.

Dependency Chain

Billing Evidence Hierarchy
→ supports Entitlement Precedence

Entitlement Precedence
→ supports Entitlement Authority

Charge Governance
→ depends on Evidence Authority

All point toward the same unresolved concern.

⸻

Root Concept Identified

Billing Decision Authority

Definition:

The governance discipline that determines whether a consequential billing decision is sufficiently:

* scoped
* evidenced
* authorized
* reconciled
* exception-aware
* attributable
* auditable

to become a canonical Nier governance outcome.

Examples:

Charge Governance asks:

“What amount is billable?”

Entitlement Authority asks:

“What access outcome is justified?”

Billing Evidence Hierarchy asks:

“Which evidence is authoritative enough to support either decision?”

Research conclusion:

Billing Decision Authority appears to be the deeper parent concern.
